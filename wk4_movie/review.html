Review of Donnie Darko

Ellie Woodmoore August 10, 2023

★★★★★

Donnie Darko is one of my favorite movies because its the type of movie you can watch over and over again. Everytime I rewatch it I create my own narrative to it. The level of awareness in this movie is what really captivates me only because I still don't understand the movie half the time. Not to mention the soundtrack of this movie also pieces it together very nicely.


If at first you don't understand the plot of the movie that's completely okay. I didn't get it all at first and the ending left me wondering, dazed, and confused. I usually like to say "The ones that get it, get it and the ones that don't, don't" and to that meaning its totally up to the viewers perception of the movie.

Trivia & Fun Facts about Donnie Darko

  • The scene where Donnie corrects Gretchen was improvised, because Jena Malone could not say the name "Professor Kenneth Monnitoff" correctly.
  • Jake Gyllenhaal uses the strategy of rarely blinking to enhance his psychotic creepiness (as he is driven by Frank).
  • Filmed over a period of 28 days - which matches the length of time depicted in the film.
